-熟词生义-

 

1. 「time」: 在文中的意思是时期，可数名词。

原句：It is in bad times that you find out who your real friends are.

time 作不可数名词有时间的意思。

例句: Their marriage got better as time went by.

time 作动词有计时；测定…的时间意思。

例句: We had to run up the stairs while the Sergeant timed us.

 

2. 「crow」: 在这篇文章里，crowing做名词意思是啼叫。

原句：...for I wake up honest men to their work in the morning by my crowing.

 

当然今天文章里也出现了crow作为乌鸦的意思哦~

原句：The crow ended the argument by saying...

 

 

－句子解析－

 

1. The crow ended the argument by saying, “Your feathers are all very well in the spring, but mine protect me against the winter.”

  • 「end the argument」end 在这里是作动词“结束，终止”的意思。ended the argument by saying…的意思就是“乌鸦通过说了一句…来结束了争论”。

  • 「all very well」这个短语是形容在某些情况下非常好或者非常有用，而暗示在另外一种情况下就不是很好或者不是很有用。这句话中就是说燕子的羽毛在春天非常好，然而在冬天却不好用。

  • 「mine」在这里是“我的”的意思，代指“我的羽毛”。英语中还可以用yours表示“你的（某物）”。

 

2. Over time the boy/ ,now a grown man, /began to steal things of greater value.

  • 「over time」 在这里指“时光慢慢流逝”。它还有一个同义短语哟~as the time goes by表示“随着时间慢慢流逝”，大家来猜猜下面两句话可以对应哪句歌词呢？

    - Over time I only care you.

    - As the time goes by, I only care you.


 

  • 「now a grown man」插入语又出现啦~这个部分是在修饰boy，指这个男孩已经是大人了。

  这句话的意思是：随着时光的流逝，男孩长大成人，开始偷更加值钱的东西（ things of greater value）了。

 

3. As he was being led away, his mother fell to her knees, crying.

  • 「as he was being led away」as在这里是“当…的时候”，be led away是被带走，be being led away是正在被带走。前半句的意思就是：“当他正被带走的时候”。

  •  「fall to one's knees」是跪下的意思。之前我们还学过一个类似的短语大家还记得吗？ get upon one’s feet 站起来。

  • 「crying」在这里是修饰他的母亲的动作，哭了。后半句的意思是他的母亲跪在地上，哭了。

 

4. You should have been as sad as this when he first stole something.

  • 「should have done」在这里的意思是“本应该做…而事实上没有做”。给大家举个例子~

    You should have finished your homework yesterday. 你本该昨天就完成你的作业的（事实上昨天没有完成）。

  •  「as sad as this」“像这次一样难过”。as...as这个句型表示“像…一样…”,给大家再举一个例子~

    You are as sweet as honey. 你像蜂蜜一样甜美。

    这句话表示：他第一次偷东西的时候，你就该像这么难过。

 

5. Some thieves broke into a house, and found nothing worth taking except a rooster, which they took away with them.

  •  「broke into a house」break into表示“破门而入”，这里就是闯入一户人家的意思。

  • 「found nothing worth taking」 是指“没有值得带的东西”。worth doing是指“值得做某事”，比如It is not       worth having such an expensive car. 拥有这样一辆昂贵的车是不值得的。

  • 「which...away with them」这个部分修饰前面的rooster，什么样的的公鸡呢？——一只值得他们带走的公鸡。

    所以这句话的整体理解就是：一群小偷集体闯进了一户人家的房子，发现除了一只他们带走的公鸡外并没有什么值得拿走的东西。

 

6. Into the pot you go!

  •  这句话在是把「into the pot」提前了，正常的语序是“You go into the pot.”。在这里把into the pot提前是为了表示强调，类似于“你进锅里去吧”。其实就是小偷们要把公鸡煮了吃。


 

7. Why did I want to fly in the clouds when I have enough trouble walking on the earth?

  •  「have enough trouble doing something」表示“做某事很难”这里就是说乌龟走起路很难。再给大家举个例子~She has enough trouble cooking. 做饭对她来说很难。

  • 「on the earth」 在这里是指“在陆地上”。还记得我们之前讲的on earth吗？表示“究竟、到底”的意思。分别给大家举个例子吧~

    There are all kinds of plants living on the earth. 陆地上生长着各种各样的植物。

    I don`t know what are you doing on earth. 我不懂你到底在干什么。

    这句话的意思是：我明明在陆地上就有这么多困难了，为什么还想着要上天呢。

那些年我们一起玩过的游戏~~~


那么今天Max带领大家踏上时光列车，开启一段怀旧之旅，来看一看那些扎根在我们记忆深处的童年游戏。


1、hopscotch（跳房子）:

Hopscotch is a children's game played on a pattern of squares marked on the ground.


Each child throws a stone into a square then hops(=jumps on one leg，单脚跳) and jumps alone the empty square to pick up the stone again.


2、shuttlecock kicking（踢毽子）：

It is game that people use two sides of the feet, kneel or tiptoe(脚尖) to hit the shuttlecock, and make the shuttlecock continuously upward. 


It is also a game that suits the tastes of both old and young, men and women.

3、marbles（打玻璃弹珠）：

Marbles is a children's game played with small balls, usually made of coloured glass. You roll a ball along the ground and try to hit an opponent's ball with it.

4、picture-story books / comic strip（连环画、小人书）

It is a series of drawings that tell a story or depict（刻画）a person and are often printed in books.

5、hide-and-seek（捉迷藏）：

Hide-and-seek is a popular children's game in which any number of players conceal（隐藏） themselves in the environment and then the seeker will try to find them.

6、Chinese jump rope/rubber band skipping（跳皮筋）：

The game is typically played by three or more players.Two of the participants face each other several feet apart. 


The third player (the jumper) stands between the two sides of the rope and must accomplish(完成) a series of difficult moves without making an error.

7、make believe/ pretend play（过家家）

It is a kind of role-play.

8、drop the handkerchief（丢手绢）：

In the game，one player runs behind the other players as they sit in a circle and drops a handkerchief behind one of the other who then must pick up the handkerchief and run around the circle and try to catch the first player.
